Who we are

We are professional photographers who are passionate about capturing beautiful memories. Our profession and passion is bound by our collective experience and emotional understanding of motherhood first hand. As mothers and daughters we value this precious bond and as professionals we possess the expertise to preserve this sacred bond as beautiful memories.

As young mothers we understand the rainbow of emotions that motherhood brings and the desire to hold on to each and every one of these beautiful memories. A baby’s first tooth is as precious a moment as the baby’s first attempt to get up from a fall and we would like to help you create picture perfect memories of all these beautiful moments.

Our experience as Photographers and Film Makers along with our understanding of this precious bond made us embark on this path of preserving precious memories.


Why Choose Us?

To create a picture of a childhood unmatched by words, to capture the fleeting moments of exuberance and innocence, to cherish the time you are spending with the joy of your life.

We are women, mothers and daughters first, and then professionals. Photography is much more to us than 'smile please', it is a moment frozen in time.

Meet the Team

Huma Qadri

Some people become mothers, some are born one - Huma belongs to the latter group. Starting her career as a News Anchor and Producer, Huma now has taken a break to focus on a bigger task at hand, motherhood.

She rejoices now in mentoring and nurturing fresh talent in the field and taking out time for her first love, photography.

Sonali

A TV producer by profession, and after working in Delhi and Mumbai with leading channels, Sonali took a break about 2 years ago to spend time with her daughter. Now 1 and a half years old, her daughter Ira is her inspiration to pick up the camera once again.

She is also teaching photography and film production at one of the most reputed Mass Comm institutes in Delhi NCR.

Farhana Numan

From learning how to wrap a saree using a bed sheet as a child imitating her mum, to now trying her recipes exactly how she has seen them, Farhana's mum has been the epitomy of unconditional love and perfection for her.

Starting her career as a journalist for a leading newspaper. Farhana is now a part of marketing team for a publishing house and in her free time, tries to recreate the candid moments of childhood by photography.
